Photographic society

Media Unit


  • Isipathana College Media Unit (ICMU) is another most active and award winning society in school. The base for the Isipathana college Media unit was put on in year 1999. Right now ICMU is a leading media club in the island.
  • In Year 2012 they did the record braking biggest school media day in Sri Lankan history at Jetliner Cruiser


  • And also ICMU is handling a FM channel named FM Vibhavi. All the programmes are conducted by media unit members and they have done several
    star programmes with the famouse celebrities in Sri Lanka including La Singnor, Dinakshie Priyasad, Fill-T, Funky Dirt, Udaya Sri etc.
